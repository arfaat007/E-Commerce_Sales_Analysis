
# E-Commerce Sales Analysis - Business Insights Report

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce sales data using SQL and identify business insights related to revenue, customers, orders, monthly trends, repeat purchases, and customer ranking.

## Key Business Questions

1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. How many orders were placed?
3. What is the average order value?
4. Who are the top customers by revenue?
5. Which months generated the highest revenue?
6. How many customers made repeat purchases?
7. How can customers be ranked based on revenue?
